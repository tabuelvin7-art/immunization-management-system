# Parent Portal User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Creating Your Parent Account

1. Visit the registration page
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Secure password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Select "Parent" as your role
3. Click "Register" to create your account

### 2. Linking Your Child's Records

After registration, you'll need to link your children's medical records:

1. Contact your healthcare provider
2. Request a verification code for each child
3. Get your child's Patient ID from the clinic
4. In the app, go to "Link Child" and enter:
   - Child's Patient ID
   - Verification code from healthcare provider

## Understanding Immunization Status

### Status Indicators
- **Completed**: Vaccination has been administered
- **Due**: Vaccination is scheduled and due
- **Overdue**: Vaccination is past due date

### Priority Levels
- **High Priority**: Overdue immunizations requiring immediate attention
- **Medium Priority**: Due within 2 weeks
- **Low Priority**: Scheduled for future dates

## Notification Management

### Types of Notifications
1. **Upcoming Immunization**: Sent 7 days before due date
2. **Overdue Immunization**: Daily reminders for missed vaccines
3. **Appointment Reminder**: Scheduled appointment confirmations
4. **General**: System updates and announcements

### Managing Notifications
- Mark individual notifications as read by clicking on them
- Use "Mark All as Read" to clear all notifications
- Filter notifications by type or priority
- Unread notifications show with a blue dot indicator

## Security & Privacy

### Data Protection
- All medical information is encrypted and secure
- Only authorized parents/guardians can access records
- Verification codes ensure account security
- Regular security updates and monitoring

### Account Security
- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out when using shared devices
- Contact support if you suspect unauthorized access

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't link my child's account:**
- Verify the Patient ID is correct
- Check that the verification code hasn't expired (usually 24-48 hours)
- Ensure you're listed as parent/guardian in medical records
- Contact your healthcare provider for a new verification code

**Not receiving notifications:**
- Check your notification settings
- Verify your email address is correct
- Look in spam/junk folders
- Contact support if issues persist

**Child's information appears incorrect:**
- Medical record updates must be made through your healthcare provider
- Contact the clinic to update patient information
- Changes will sync to the parent portal within 24 hours

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often are notifications sent?**
A: Upcoming immunization notifications are sent 7 days before due date. Overdue notifications are sent daily until resolved.

**Q: Can I link multiple children?**
A: Yes, you can link as many children as needed. Each child requires a separate verification process.

**Q: What if I change healthcare providers?**
A: Contact your new provider to transfer records and get new verification codes for the parent portal.

**Q: Is my child's medical information secure?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and follows healthcare privacy regulations. Only authorized users can access records.

**Q: Can both parents access the same child's records?**
A: Each parent needs their own account and verification code from the healthcare provider.

**Q: What happens if I miss an immunization deadline?**
A: Contact your healthcare provider immediately to reschedule. The system will continue sending overdue notifications until resolved.
